Pilis
Barber Shop

Pilis’s Barber Shop was established 20 years ago with the single mission to serve
the gentleman client by offering only the best techniques, service and
products available. Today, Pilis’s Barber Shop is widely recognized as the
barbershop of choice in the Boulder/Denver area, and continues to set the
highest standards of excellence in men’s haircutting, styling and grooming.

Founded by Muhammad Pilis in 1995, Pilis’s Barber Shop believes in the art and
craft of men’s grooming and exceptional, personalized service. Designed to be
a welcoming environment where men can feel comfortable, Pilis’s Barber Shop
offers classic, old-school haircutting and grooming techniques blended with
today’s styles and trends. It’s a place where you are warmly greeted – by name
– and where you will be well taken care of. Once seated in one of the handsome
barber chairs, you can rest easy knowing you will receive your complete
service in one spot – no ushering from place to place. We want you to kick
back, relax and enjoy yourself.
